About Team TechnoSharks

Team TechnoSharks is a passionate group of students dedicated to robotics excellence and STEM outreach. Founded in 2020, we compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge, where we design, build, and program robots to complete challenging tasks.

Our mission extends beyond competition - we're committed to inspiring younger students, promoting STEM education in our community, and developing the problem-solving skills that will drive tomorrow's innovations.
